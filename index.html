What is The ToyBox Project?

The Toybox Project is a fun-first game experience, brought to life through the world of Minecraft, extensive Fabric coding, and of course, the story telling of our community. Our staff team, composed of specialists with backgrounds in all sorts of digital media, aims to create a unique experience that blends the aspect of human imagination into a space that can be enjoyed by all.

What do we have to offer?

Magesitc Beasts And Monsters

A plethora of creatures have invaded the many worlds we call home! Hunt, tame, or even work alongside the numerous entities that inhabit these lands, but be careful, as one wrong step might be the end of your life!

Various Blocks And Furnitures

Home is where the heart is, so every home deserves to be just as beautiful as the heart living inside! Explore the many colorful and delicately crafted building resources, all right at your fingertips for creating a masterpiece!

Delectable Foods And Drinks

With all of the confusion in the world, why not take a moment to relax with a cold drink and a warm meal? With the multitude of accessible food related resources, no player will get exhausted of having to eat the same meal over and over again!

Powerful Tools And Equipment

As if the consequences of the industrial revolution weren't enough already, we found a way to add even more spice into reshaping the world you inhabit! If one person can turn over a continent with a shovel, imagine what a jackhammer does!
